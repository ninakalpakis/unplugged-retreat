Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

To get to the Unplugged retreat, simply take the first exit off of I-10, and then a left once you reach the red barn. Drive 4 miles north, and the Unplugged retreat will be on your right next to the bee hive.

What size teams can you accomodate?

We can acccomodate teams as small as 4, and as large as 50. For larger groups, please make sure to book at least six weeks in advance.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Any team! We love to work with teams on all levels, and enjoy facilitating conversations with new teams, as well as continuing to foster connections with more established teams. Many of our members return year after year, and rave about the amazing things we can do to strengthen team bonds.

Is there really no wifi available?

Correct - kind of. We strongly encourage team members to truly unplug while attending our retreat. This is why no wifi is available in the cabins and main areas of our facilities. However, we understand that sometimes, duty calls! Therefore, we do provide an office with wifi connection for instances where you do need it, with the hope that you use it with reservation.
